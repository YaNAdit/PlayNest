PlayNest

Where Little Adventures Begin!

PlayNest is a vibrant, safe, and nurturing indoor play center that inspires joy, creativity, and confidence in children. We offer a modern, dynamic, and engaging atmosphere where kids can explore, learn, and grow through play.

Soft Play Zone

For younger children (2-5 years) with padded flooring, soft blocks, and climbing structures.

Adventure Play Area

For older children (6-12 years), featuring climbing walls, slides, rope courses, and obstacle courses.

Imagination Corner

A creative space for role-play, arts and crafts, and storytelling activities.

Sensory Play Zone

Interactive sensory activities with tactile objects, light displays, and sensory walls.

Educational Workshops

Learning through play with arts and crafts, storytelling, and interactive programs.

Birthday Party Rooms

Themed, private rooms customizable for parties and special events.

Parent Lounge

A comfortable space with Wi-Fi and refreshments for parents to relax while supervising.

Safe and Secure Environment

Childproof equipment, CCTV monitoring, trained staff, and constant supervision.
